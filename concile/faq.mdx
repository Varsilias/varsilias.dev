## Is Concile a ledger?

No. Concile compares records. Your ledger remains the source of accounting truth.

## Can Concile reconcile CSV files?

Yes, if the records can be normalized into a stable shape. File adapters should preserve enough metadata to explain where records came from.

## Should reconciliation rules be strict?

Start strict. Loosen rules only when the business reason is clear and documented.

## What does disputed mean?

Disputed means records appear related but differ beyond the configured rule tolerance.

## Is Concile stable?

No. Treat Concile as experimental until the API and rule model are verified against released packages.
