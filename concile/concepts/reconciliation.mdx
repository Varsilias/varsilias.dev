Reconciliation compares two views of the same business event. Concile turns that comparison into outcomes that can be reviewed.

## Outcomes

| Outcome | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Matched | Records agree under the configured rule |
| Unmatched | A record exists on one side but not the other |
| Disputed | Records are related but differ beyond the allowed tolerance |

## Matching

A match should be explainable. Prefer stable references, amounts, currencies, account IDs, and settlement windows over fuzzy text matching.

## Review

Not every unmatched record is an error. Some are timing differences, pending settlements, or expected retries. Review workflows should preserve decisions so repeated runs become easier to interpret.
