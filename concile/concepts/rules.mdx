---
title: "Rules"
description: "Define Concile matching rules that stay deterministic, reviewable, and safe."
---

Rules decide whether records represent the same business event. Keep them deterministic and easy to explain.

## Rule anatomy

```yaml
name: settlement-reference-match
fields:
  - amount
  - currency
  - settlement_reference
tolerance:
  amount: 0
```

## Good rules

- Use stable fields.
- Define amount tolerances explicitly.
- Keep rule names tied to business meaning.
- Produce enough detail for review.

## Risky rules

- Fuzzy matching without review.
- Matching on mutable names or descriptions.
- Ignoring currency or account boundaries.
- Hiding amount differences under broad tolerances.
